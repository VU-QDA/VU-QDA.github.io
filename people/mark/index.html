EFRO Smart Maintenance

In this project, funded by the EU (EFRO), the goal is to use and improve machine learning techniques for predictive maintenance and optimization of maintenance.

Efficient Deep Learning

In this project, funded by NWO (TTW), the goal is to improve the efficiency of deep learning algorithms.

PIPPI

This project concerns the identification of risk models for mental health problems among children and adolescents using data from general practitioners and the municipality health services.

RESCUED

The identification of risk factors for cardiac arrest among diabetes patients is the main goal of this project.

Predictive Modeling for Outlier Detection

Predictive modeling for outlier detection for large datasets.

Machine Learning for Intensive Care

This project aims to develop predictive models based on Electronic Medical Records for the Intensive Care department at the VU University Medical Center using machine learning techniques.

Constrained Personalization of Smart Health Apps

In this project, we aim to develop techniques to personalize within certain boundaries.

Personalized Smart Health Apps

This project aims to develop techniques to learn to provide feedback and support to users in an effective way by means of health apps on their smart phone.

Reinforcement Learning in Regulated Domains

This project is aimed at bridging the gap between regulators’ and agent’s behaviour representation and reinforcement learning under the resulting constraints.

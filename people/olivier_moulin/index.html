Olivier Moulin

PhD. student

Olivier Moulin is a PhD student in the Quantitative Data Analytics group of the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam (VU). He is under the supervision of Mark Hoogendoorn (Quantitative Data Analytics, VU). Olivier’s PhD research is mainly on Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning on structured data applied to ICU patient data.

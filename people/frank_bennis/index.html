Frank Bennis

Postdoc

Frank Bennis started in September 2020 as a postdoctoral researcher in the Quantitative Data Analytics group under supervision of dr. Mark Hoogendoorn. He focuses on the identification of parameters and subsequently the prediction of Sudden Cardiac Death in patients based on general practicioner (GP) data. He furthermore collaborates with the Nederlands Instituut voor Onderzoek van de Gezondheidszorg for the prediction of multiple cardiac diseases from GP data. With his background in Technical Medicine, he aims to establish the connection between data science and medicine.

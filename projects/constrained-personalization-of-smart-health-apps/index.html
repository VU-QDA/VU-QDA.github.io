Constrained Personalization of Smart Health Apps

Constrained Personalization of Smart Health Apps

In this project, we aim to develop techniques to personalize within certain boundaries.

When personalization of apps on smart phones takes place automatically it is possible that certain undesired behavior is shown, e.g. inappropriate feedback or suggestions for interventions. This could be harmful for users which needs to be avoided. In this project, we aim to develop techniques to personalize within certain boundaries. We also consider this from a software architecture perspective and also study the consequences for the users. This project is a collaboration with the Software and Services Group of the VU (Patricia Lago and Ivano Malavolta).
